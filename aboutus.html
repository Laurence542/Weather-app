About us


We're a team of meteorologists, data scientists, and technology experts who are passionate about providing accurate and reliable weather forecasts to people around the world. Our mission is to help people stay safe and informed about the weather, whether they're planning a trip, working outdoors, or just want to know what to wear each day. Our website was founded in 2022, and since then we've grown to become one of the most trusted sources of weather information on the internet. We use the latest technologies and data sources to deliver forecasts that are reliable and easy to understand, and we're always working to improve and expand our services. Our team is made up of experienced meteorologists who have dedicated their careers to studying and predicting the weather. We also have a talented group of data scientists and engineers who work to ensure that our website is fast, reliable, and easy to use.
